This is the American Alligator,


Male and female alligators have an "armored" body with a muscular flat tail, used to propel them forward while swimming. The skin on their back is armored with embedded bony plates called "osteoderms" or "scutes." Adult alligators are olive brown or black with a creamy white underside.
