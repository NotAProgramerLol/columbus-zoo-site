This is the Bald Eagle,


Sexually mature bald eagles have the distinctive white head. The juveniles are a mottled brown until reaching sexual maturity at three to four years of age. Both adult male and female eagles have a dark brown to black body and wings, set off by a pure white head and tail, large curved yellow bill, and yellow feet.
